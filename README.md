# PROJECT OVERVIEW
___
### HealthConnect is a Hospital Management System (HMS) designed specifically for the Pediatrics Department of QSUT
(Qendra Spitalore Universitare "Nënë Tereza"), the largest public hospital in Albania, located in Tirana.
The system aims to digitize and streamline the department's daily operations, from patient registration 
and bed management to doctor assignments and discharge summaries, replacing outdated paper-based workflows with an integrated, 
reliable digital platform.

## PROBLEM STATEMENT
___
The Pediatrics Department at QSUT currently faces several operational challenges:  

- manual patient registration leads to data duplication and errors  
- there is no centralized tracking of bed availability and ward occupancy  
- paper-based medical records make it difficult to access patient history quickly  
- poor coordination exists between doctors, nurses, and administrative staff  
- there is a lack of reporting tools to monitor department performance  
- long waiting times occur due to unstructured appointment and triage processes  

HealthConnect addresses these problems 
by providing a unified digital system tailored to the specific workflows of a public pediatric hospital department in Albania.

## SYSTEM FEATURES AND USERS
___
### Main Users

The main users of the system include:

- **Administrators** – manage the system and user accounts
- **Doctors** – update medical records and patient treatments
- **Nurses** – assist with patient information and monitor hospital beds
- **Receptionists** – handle patient registration and appointment scheduling
- **Users (Patients)** – access their basic medical information and appointments

### Key System Features

The system includes several important functionalities:

- Patient registration and management
- Bed management and monitoring
- Doctor assignment to patients
- Medical records management
- Patient discharge summaries

## TECHNICAL OVERVIEW
___
HealthConnect will be a web-based system accessible through a browser within the hospital network. It will use
a client–server architecture, where the front-end interface allows staff to manage patients, beds, and medical records, 
while the back-end processes requests and handles system logic. All data will be stored in a centralized database,
ensuring fast access to patient information and better data organization. The system will also include user authentication 
and role-based access control so that each type of user (administrator, doctor, nurse, receptionist) can only access the functions 
relevant to their role.

## STAKEHOLDERS
___
The main **stakeholders** of the HealthConnect system include the hospital administration, doctors, nurses, receptionists,
patients, and the IT support team at Qendra Spitalore Universitare "Nënë Tereza". Hospital administrators use the system to 
monitor operations and manage resources. Doctors and nurses use it to manage patient information, treatments, and medical records.
Receptionists handle patient registration and scheduling. Patients and their parents benefit from faster
and more organized healthcare services. The IT support team is responsible for maintaining the system 
and ensuring its security and proper functionality.
